How this works

Everything you change here saves straight to your Supabase database. Your public site (index.html / blog.html) reads live from that same database, so changes appear for every visitor immediately — no rebuild or redeploy needed. Only your signed-in Supabase account can write; the public pages only have read access.

How your login actually works

This admin panel uses real Supabase Authentication. Your password is hashed and stored securely on Supabase's servers — it is never written in any file, ever visible in your site's code, or readable by anyone viewing your public pages. Only someone who knows your email + password can sign in here. To add another admin user, or reset your password, go to your Supabase Dashboard → Authentication → Users.
